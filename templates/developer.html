CareerGuidance — Platform Features

Job Listings Portal

Browse curated job openings across India and Tamil Nadu — entry-level, fresher, and experienced roles updated regularly.

Smart Job Search

Filter and search jobs by keyword, location, category, or experience level with a fast, intuitive search interface.

Region-Specific Jobs

Dedicated sections for India-wide and Tamil Nadu–specific jobs, making local career discovery seamless.

Candidate Profiles

Students can register, create profiles, and submit their resumes directly through the platform to potential employers.

Employer Dashboard

Companies and recruiters can post job openings and manage applications with a clean, dedicated employer interface.

Tech Articles & Blogs

Career advice, interview preparation tips, and industry news curated for students and fresh graduates.

Admin Control Panel

A powerful dark-themed admin dashboard to manage users, job posts, signups, platform health, and site configuration.

Mobile Responsive

Fully responsive design optimised for mobile, tablet, and desktop — accessible anywhere, any device.

SEO Optimised

Comprehensive sitemap, meta tags, and keyword strategy ensuring strong visibility on Google for job searchers.
