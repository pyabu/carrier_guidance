About CareerPath Pro

Empowering Indian students, freshers, and professionals through technology, guidance, and opportunity

🚀 Our Mission

Bridging the Gap Between Indian Talent & Opportunity

CareerPath Pro was founded with a simple but powerful vision: to democratize career guidance and job access for every Indian student and professional — whether you're a B.Tech fresher in a Tier-3 college, an IIT graduate aiming for FAANG, or an experienced professional switching careers.

India produces 15 lakh+ engineering graduates every year, yet most lack proper career guidance. We combine AI-powered technology with industry expertise to provide personalized career roadmaps, real-time job scraping from top Indian & global platforms, resume building tools tailored for the Indian market, and interview preparation resources for campus placements and beyond.
