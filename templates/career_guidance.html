🎤 Interview Prep

Crack Any Interview

Proven strategies and tips to ace technical and behavioral interviews

Technical Interview Guide

Data Structures & Algorithms: Master arrays, trees, graphs, DP. Practice on LeetCode, HackerRank.

System Design: Learn scalability, databases, caching, load balancing. Read "Designing Data-Intensive Apps".

Coding Tips: Always clarify requirements, think aloud, test edge cases, optimize iteratively.

Behavioral Interview Guide

STAR Method: Structure answers as Situation → Task → Action → Result.

Common Questions: "Tell me about yourself", "Why this company?", "Greatest strength/weakness?"

Tips: Research the company, prepare 5-7 stories, show enthusiasm, ask thoughtful questions.

Interview Day Checklist

✅ Research the company and interviewer
✅ Review the job description thoroughly
✅ Prepare questions to ask them
✅ Test your tech setup (for virtual interviews)
✅ Dress professionally
✅ Arrive 10 minutes early
✅ Bring copies of your resume
✅ Follow up with a thank-you email
